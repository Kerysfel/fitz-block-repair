# Code of Conduct

We expect all participants to be respectful and professional.

## Our Standards

- Be kind and constructive.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Keep discussions focused on the work.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer at:
vershinin@itmo.ru

The maintainer may take any action deemed appropriate, including removing content or restricting access.
